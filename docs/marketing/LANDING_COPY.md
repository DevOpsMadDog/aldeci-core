## Solution Section

### Three Pillars. One Platform.

**Pillar 1: Unified Data Plane**
TrustGraph aggregates 28 threat intelligence feeds + 32 scanner normalizers into a single knowledge graph. Snyk report? Wiz scan? Nessus asset? All normalized, deduplicated, and correlated in seconds. Works air-gapped. Works with everything you already own.

**Pillar 2: AI Consensus, Not Guesswork**
Multi-LLM council (Claude, GPT, open models) votes on every vulnerability. 3+ LLMs. 85% majority threshold. No single-model hallucinations. No vendor lock-in. Findings scored by exploitability (MPTE 19-phase pen-test engine) — not just detected, but proven.

**Pillar 3: 30 Personas. One UI.**
- **CISO:** Board-ready risk dashboard, compliance scorecard, trend analysis
- **SOC Analyst:** Prioritized incidents, evidence bundles, auto-generated playbooks
- **DevSecOps:** Remediation lanes, PR generation, automated fixes
- **Auditor:** Quantum-secure evidence (FIPS 204 ML-DSA), 7-year WORM retention

- **Plus 25 more:** Every persona, one login.

## How It Works (3-Step Flow)

**1. Ingest** → All your scanners (Snyk, Burp, Nessus, SonarQube, ...) pipe findings into ALdeci. Zero rip-and-replace. Works day 1.

**2. Decide** → Multi-LLM consensus + MPTE exploit verification + risk scoring. Top 5 findings bubble up. 90% noise filtered.

**3. Fix** → AI-powered remediation: PR generation, auto-apply patches (with confidence scores), compliance proof generated automatically.
